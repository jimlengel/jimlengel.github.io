Videography Techniques

Creating a short movie can be done well if you follow a few tips. Here are some ideas with illustrations that may help you.

Techniques with Illustrations:

  1. Start strong. Start with an opening shot that introduces your video.
  2. Follow the action. Be as close to the action as possible. The first image is a great shot, but the second image is closer to the action.
